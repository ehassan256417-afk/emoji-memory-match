How to Play

  1. Enter your name and choose a difficulty, then click Save & Apply.
  2. Click Play / Reset to shuffle and deal the cards.
  3. Click any card to flip it and reveal the emoji.
  4. Click a second card. If the emojis match, they stay face-up!
  5. If they do not match, both cards flip back after a short pause.
  6. Match all pairs to win. Fewer attempts = higher score!

Tip: Your best score is saved automatically.
